We are a collective for humor, empowering an array of creative perspectives and voices. National Lampoon acquires, develops, finances and produces all formats – from short form web series content, to major studio feature films and TV series.

Netflix announced its 2018 release of feature film “A Futile and Stupid Gesture,” adapted from the best selling 2006 book by Josh Karp that chronicles the life and career of National Lampoon co-founder Doug Kenney. Netflix will release A Futile and Stupid Gesture in 2018.
